The blue plot added one extra predictor variable (sepal length), and the green plot added two (sepal length and sepal width), which led the difference in residuals. In the blue plot I noticed that some of the residuals were closer to the true value, and even more so in the green plot, which could mean that adding those variables possibly leads to more accurate preditions.
